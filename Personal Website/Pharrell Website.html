Bio

Pharrell Lanscilo Williams is an American singer, rapper, songwriter, record producer, and fashion designer. He is best known for being the lead vocalist of the band N.E.R.D, which he formed with friends in the early-1990s, and his work as a featured artist on several hit singles such as "Get Lucky" and "Happy".
